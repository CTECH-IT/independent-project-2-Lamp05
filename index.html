Quarry Hill Nature Center

Nature Center

  • The "Exploration Hall" is a large room within the Nature center where you can learn about animals and fossils. It's a very hands-on experience, with many of the fossils and other pieces of nature laid out on tables, intended to be handled and examined.

  • Quarry Hill has an extensive aquarium, displaying many different fish and turtles that you can watch and read about. Some of the turtles even have names, which I find especially adorable.
